Project Unicorn Magic

A Recap of Sisi's Magical Experience

✨ Executive Summary

Over the course of this interaction, we transformed a simple idea—a magical conversation with unicorns for a little girl named Sisi—into a sophisticated, high-fidelity AI application. Our journey spanned complex technical migrations, innovative UI design, and creative problem-solving to ensure the experience remained magical and accessible. The final result is a world-class, standalone AI tool capable of speech, emotional expression, and interactive play.
